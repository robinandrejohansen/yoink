02

Why local

Every online downloader is a trap of ads, fake buttons, malware, and 480p caps. Yoink runs on your machine, so it just works, stays private, and is yours.

Cloud servers also get blocked by YouTube within minutes, which is why hosted downloaders break constantly. Local means it keeps working.
